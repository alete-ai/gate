Privacy in the Age of AI

Posted on June 12, 2025

As large language models become ubiquitous, the question of data sovereignty has never been more pressing.

We must consider how our personal information is ingested, processed, and stored by these digital giants.

The Importance of the Edge

Edge-based computing offers a potential solution by keeping sensitive data on the user's device.

By filtering information before it ever reaches the cloud, we can protect our most intimate details.
